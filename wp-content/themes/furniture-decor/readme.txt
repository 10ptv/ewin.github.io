== Description ==

Furniture Decor is an exquisite and versatile WordPress theme tailored specifically for furniture stores, interior designers, modular kitchen shop, Bed & Tables, and home decor businesses. The visually appealing design of this theme captivates visitors from the moment they land on your website. The theme offers a wide range of customization options, allowing you to personalize colors, fonts, and layouts to reflect your brand identity and create a unique online presence. The carefully crafted design elements and attention to detail enhance the overall aesthetic appeal of your website, making it stand out in the competitive market. Functionality is a key strength of the Prime Furniture Decor WordPress Theme. It includes features such as product galleries, user-friendly navigation, and seamless integration with popular e-commerce plugins like WooCommerce. This enables you to effortlessly showcase your furniture collections, manage orders, and provide a smooth online shopping experience for your customers. The theme also includes a responsive design, ensuring that your website looks stunning and functions flawlessly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Prime Furniture Decor offers advanced features to enhance the user experience and streamline website management. It includes options for creating interactive product showcases, integrating customer reviews, and incorporating social media feeds to engage with your audience. The theme is also optimized for search engines, improving your website's visibility and attracting organic traffic.

== Installation ==
        
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
